Frequently Asked Questions

Check out our FAQ's below to see if your question has been answered. If you can't find the answer you're looking for, feel free to Contact Us !

  • How do I start using the chatbot?

    To start using the chatbot, simply click on the chat icon located at the bottom right corner of the screen. This will open the chat interface where you can type your message to begin interacting with the chatbot.

  • What can the chatbot help me with?

    The chatbot can assist you with various tasks such as retrieving stock information, making price predictions, answering general inquiries about stocks, and performing comparisons between different stocks based on various metrics.

  • Is the chatbot available 24/7?

    Yes, the chatbot is available 24/7 for your convenience. You can access it anytime to get assistance or information related to stocks.

  • How do I report an issue or give feedback about the chatbot?

    You can report any issues or provide feedback about the chatbot by clicking on the "Report Conversation" button located in the chat interface. This will allow you to submit your feedback or report any issues directly to the system.

  • Can the chatbot handle specific types of inquiries or requests?

    Yes, the chatbot is equipped to handle specific types of inquiries or requests related to stocks. It can address factual questions, make price predictions, perform comparisons, and provide information on over 9000 stocks and cryptocurrencies.

  • What languages does the chatbot support?

    The chatbot currently supports interactions in English language only.

  • How do I change my preferences or settings in the chatbot?

    To change your preferences or settings in the chatbot, you can access the settings menu by clicking on the menu icon (if available) in the chat interface. From there, you can adjust various preferences such as theme, language (if supported), and notification settings.

  • Is my data secure with the chatbot?

    Yes, your data is secure with the chatbot. The system ensures secure access and data handling through mechanisms such as authentication, password management, and secure data storage using PostgreSQL.

  • Can I escalate to a human agent if needed?

    Unfortunately, the chatbot does not currently support escalation to a human agent. However, you can report any issues or concerns using the provided reporting functionality, and the appropriate action will be taken to address them.

  • How do I end the conversation with the chatbot?

    To end the conversation with the chatbot, simply close the chat interface by clicking on the close button located at the top right corner of the chat window. Alternatively, you can type a message indicating that you wish to end the conversation, and the chatbot will acknowledge and conclude the interaction.
