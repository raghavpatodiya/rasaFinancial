About Chatbot

This chatbot is built using Rasa, Python Flask, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript technologies. It combines machine learning and web development to deliver a robust and interactive experience.

Chatbot Components

  • Rasa Model & Actions
  • Flask Application
  • Templates ( HTML, CSS & JS )
  • Flask Migrations & PostgreSQL - PGAdmin4
  • Python Automation Scripts
  • requirements.txt
  • Rasa Model Test Results

Chatbot Features

  • Compare stock price, market sentiment, volatility, stock trend, market cap, revenue, EPS, PE ratio, ROE, gross margins, DTE ratio
  • Get market top gainers and losers
  • Process stock trend graph, ROE graph, revenue graph, volume graph, revenue per share graph, return on assets graph, earnings growth graph, revenue growth graph, gross margins graph, EBITDA margins graph, operating margins graph
  • Retrieve stock currency, number of employees, location, website, industry, sector, description, leadership team, market cap, previous close, day open, day low, day high, audit risk, board risk, compensation risk, shareholders risk, overall risk, governance epoch date, compensation as of epoch date, max age, price hint, beta, PE ratio, volume, bid size, ask size, 52 weeks low, 52 weeks high, 50-day average, 200-day average, enterprise value, profit margins, float shares, shares outstanding, shares short, book value, price to book, etc.
  • Price format functions, epoch to date functions
  • Price predictions for the next 7 days using time series XGBoost model
  • Use linear regression to predict day close of a stock
  • Get stock price as well as older stock price
  • Retrieve market sentiment of a stock
  • Retrieve market status
  • Post predictions, buy sell or hold
  • Map functions to get stock ticker from company name / ticker
  • Get stock trend, like price trends, volatility
  • Answer factual questions on stocks
  • Rasa NLU, stories, rules, domain, config, credentials, endpoints, slots, entities, and model, test results
